
Table of content adding in word: -
=====================================

I. Create document with different headings and choose one heading and
   convert it into Heading1, Heading 2 or so i.e. level of heading.
   
II. Then keep the cursor at first page and click on References menu and 
    then select "Table of contents".

III. We can modify table of content. Click on Table of content -> Custom table 
     of content menu.

IV. While preparing document if some more headings are added, then we need to
    update the table. For this click on "Table of contents" in first page of word,
    then click on "Update table".


How to disable spelling check in word: -
=========================================
File -> Options -> Proofing -> Uncheck spelling check
